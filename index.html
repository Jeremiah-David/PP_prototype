Preparing Your Child for Success

Pioneer Preparatory School

We are a preparatory charter school that provides a safe and supportive environment for pre-elementary aged kids from under-served communities.

Welcome to the Home of the Lions!

Pioneer Preparatory School: A Challenge Foundation Academy is a tuition-free, public charter school in Phoenix, Arizona’s Maryvale neighborhood serving grades PreK–6.

Our Mission/Nuestra Misión

The mission of Pioneer Preparatory School is to provide a rigorous and relevant curriculum with balanced support from staff, teachers, family, and community—providing scholars with the knowledge and wisdom to be self-sustaining members of our society. Scholars will meet and exceed academic standards ensuring their success as lifelong learners.

La misión de la Escuela Preparatoria Pioneer es de proveer un currículum riguroso y relevante con el apoyo equilibrado del personal, los maestros, las familias, y la comunidad–proporcionándoles a los estudiantes el conocimiento y la sabiduría para ser miembros auto-suficientes de la sociedad. Los estudiantes alcanzarán y excederán las normas académicas asegurando su éxito de alcanzar su potencial.
